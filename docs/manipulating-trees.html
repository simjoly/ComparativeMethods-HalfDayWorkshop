18 Manipulating trees

There are several manipulations that can be made to trees. Here are a few examples.

18.1 Drop tips

plot(drop.tip(tree,c("t1","t2","t3","t4","t5")))

18.2 Reroot trees

plot(root(tree,"t1"))
